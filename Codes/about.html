The Taste of Korea, Right to Your Door

Our Story

Seoul Bite is a Korean ghost kitchen bringing authentic Korean flavors directly to customers' doorsteps. As a delivery-only restaurant, we focus entirely on online ordering and delivery services.

Our passion for Korean cuisine drives us to prepare each dish with the same care and authenticity you would find on the streets of Seoul. From our signature Bibimbap to our crispy Korean Fried Chicken, every item is crafted to deliver an unforgettable culinary experience.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional Korean cuisine that captures the essence of traditional Korean cooking while providing a convenient and accessible dining experience for our customers.

Our Vision

To become the leading Korean ghost kitchen in Jamaica, recognized for our commitment to authenticity, quality, and customer satisfaction.
